A [TaskRabbit Clone](https://hyperlocalcloud.com/taskrabbit-clone) is an on-demand service marketplace platform that connects customers with skilled local professionals for everyday tasks such as home cleaning, furniture assembly, moving assistance, handyman services, electrical work, plumbing, gardening, and more. The platform enables users to quickly find trusted service providers, book appointments, make secure payments, and track job progress in real time.

Designed for entrepreneurs and businesses looking to launch a hyperlocal service marketplace, this solution offers a scalable architecture, modern user experience, and customizable features to meet the needs of different business models. Whether you're building a local handyman app or a multi-service marketplace, this clone provides a strong foundation for rapid deployment and business growth.

**Features**
------------

### **Customer App**

*   User registration and secure authentication
    
*   Search nearby service providers
    
*   Browse services by category
    
*   Real-time booking and scheduling
    
*   Live provider tracking
    
*   Multiple payment options
    
*   Booking history
    
*   Ratings and reviews
    
*   Push notifications
